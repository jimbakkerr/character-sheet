competencies

Nooit goed.

I have a problem where I find it hard to actually like my own work. Perfectionism. This always strives me to do better.

Spoiled.

I’ve found it hard to let my parents help me with my work. Maybe I should more often. But it also allows me not to be dependent on my parents’ knowledge and connections.

Bad saver, good liver?.

I am really bad with money. Can’t save. This makes me good at living in the moment and not worrying to much about the future.

Multitasker.
